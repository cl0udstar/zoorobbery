Mission 5

Accessibility

Oh dear, there is a digital lock on the building and a sign outside says it is the Commercial Fish Feeding supplies Ltd.

It appears to be a digital cipher and we must decipher the code before entering inside. I bet the code has something to do with fish, or maybe even penguins.

There's a piece of paper on the ground with some data on it. Let's see if you can sort each column of data into decending order on the approximate number of each species in the world.
